Which is better Coupe or convertible?

Both of these kinds of vehicles are significantly distinct even though they share many characteristics, such as size and design. The fundamental distinction between a coupe and a convertible is that the latter has a mechanism for retracting the roof, whereas the former does not. Coupes and convertibles typically have one or two rows of seats.
